Debit Freeze Account

To debit freeze your Account, all you need is to type the following text and send an SMS to 8828837411 from your registered mobile number - Block < space > your 14 digits account number

ATM/Debit Card Block/Unlock

Hotlisting (Permanent Blocking) your Debit Card in case Lost/Stolen by simply sending an SMS. To block your Debit Card, type the text in following format and send to 9223815844 from your registered mobile number

LOST < space > last 4 digits of your card number
eg: LOST 1234


Temporary Block & Unblock Debit Card, Cardholder need to type the text in the following format and send to 9223815844 from his/her registered mobile number


For blocking, TBLOCK < space > last 4 digits of your Debit Card Number
eg: TBLOCK 1234


For unblocking, UBLOCK < space > last 4 digits of your Debit Card Number
eg: UBLOCK 1234
